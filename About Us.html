About Us

Purpose

ClearSphere provides historical air quality data in real-time, to provide users a way to monitor pollution levels and make informed decisions.

Importance of Monitoring Air Quality

Monitoring air quality is essential to protect public health. This can help identify harmful pollutants that can cause respiratory and cardiovascular issues.
